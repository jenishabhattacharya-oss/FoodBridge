How It Works

Three simple steps to get food from event to plate.

1. Post Surplus

Event organizers log available food including quantity, type, location, and pickup window.

2. Auto Match

Our system instantly notifies nearby verified NGOs, shelters, and food banks.

3. Pickup and Deliver

Volunteers or NGO teams collect and distribute the food, fully tracked in the app.
